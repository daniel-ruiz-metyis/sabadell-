# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Executive leadership at Banco Sabadell: C-suite, regional directors, and board-level stakeholders. They review the dashboard in board meetings, in brief moments between other work, and occasionally on a tablet while traveling. They are time-constrained and non-technical about the underlying systems; they need a trustworthy read on bank-wide operational health, not a data-exploration tool. The job to be done: absorb the state of the bank's operations in seconds, then decide where to dig deeper or where to direct attention.

## Product Purpose

A bank-wide operations dashboard that surfaces the KPIs executives use to steer the institution: balances, transaction volumes, branch and digital-channel performance, risk and compliance signals, and customer growth, in a single trustworthy view. It exists so leadership can make faster, better-informed strategic decisions without waiting on ad hoc reports from operations teams. Success looks like: an executive can state the bank's operational health in one sentence after ten seconds on the screen, and knows exactly where to click if that sentence needs a caveat.

## Brand Personality

Modern, efficient, clear. This is a 140-year-old institution (founded 1881, IBEX 35, one of Spain's largest banking groups) expressed through a contemporary digital lens: confident restraint rather than visual spectacle. Every element on screen earns its place. The tone is precise and calm, never flashy, never cluttered, the same institutional trust the Banco Sabadell brand signals in its wordmark and blue mark.

## Anti-references

Generic SaaS dashboard clichés: gradient hero metrics, glassmorphism, identical icon-plus-heading card grids, dark-mode-by-default fintech styling, decorative gradient text. Also avoid the opposite failure mode: dense, cluttered legacy core-banking terminal aesthetics that bury signals in undifferentiated tables. Avoid playful consumer-app styling (bright multi-color palettes, mascot-like illustration, bouncy motion) that would undermine institutional trust at executive level.

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity over decoration.** Every visual element must earn its place; if it doesn't aid a decision, cut it.
2. **Executive-glance density.** Top-level signals must be legible in under five seconds; supporting detail is available on demand through progressive disclosure, never dumped all at once.
3. **Institutional confidence, not flash.** Authority comes from precision and restraint, consistent with a bank of Banco Sabadell's scale and history, not from visual spectacle.
4. **One accent, deliberately used.** The brand blue is a signal, not a wash across the interface; its scarcity is what gives it meaning.
5. **Numbers you can trust.** Data is presented so it can be verified at a glance: consistent scales, tabular figures, no chart-junk, no ambiguous axes.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA minimum, standard for a regulated financial institution. Risk and status indicators must never rely on color alone (red/green) given color-blind users; pair color with icon, label, or pattern. Fully keyboard-navigable. Designed first for standard desktop/laptop resolutions used in boardroom and office settings, with graceful tablet support for travel use.
